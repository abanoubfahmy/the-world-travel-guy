What Is Angkor Wat? What Is The History?

Where Is Angkor Wat?

The Angkor Wat is located on the outskirts of Siem Reap, in northwest Cambodia.

The closest airport is Siem Reap-Angkor International Airport (SAI), which is served by a bunch of budget friendly airlines. Among other places, there are flights from Singapore (SIN), Bangkok (DMK), or Kuala Lumpur (KUL) to Siem Reap for as low as $40 USD. You can shop for flights to Siem Reap at Skyscanner.

You can also travel to Angkor Wat Cambodia overland from Thailand.

Most tours of the Angkor Wat temple start with a visit to the ticket office, where all foreign visitors are required to buy tickets.
