Where Is Labengki Island?

Labengki is located in Southeast Sulawesi, Indonesia.

There‚Äôs actually two islands: Labengki Kecil (small) and Labengki Besar (big). Most of the population is in a fishing village on Kecil.

Sombori actually sits on the edge of Central Sulawesi province, but it can be visited pretty easily on a day trip from Labengki.

How To Get To Labengki Island

Labengki and Sombori are remote islands, so the process of getting there takes a bit of effort.

The first step is to fly to the city of Kendari (KDI), which requires a transit stop in Makassar (UPG). Makassar is easy to reach from Bali or Jakarta, and it has non-stop daily flights to Kendari for as low as 800k Rupiah ($50 USD). You can shop for flights to Kendari at Skyscanner.

Once you arrive in Kendari, you may have to stay a night depending on when you get there. If so, we‚Äôd recommend the Swiss-Belhotel or Claro Hotel Kendari.

Where To Stay

We stayed at the Labengki Beach Huts on the big island (Labengki Besar) during our trip to this area.

The location is magic: 10 little beach huts sit at the bottom of the steep cliffs, and you have a private beach right in front of your room. Another white sand beach (Pantai Pasir Panjang) can be reached in 10 minutes of hiking.

The food was fantastic and there was lots of variety in every meal. We had fresh fish, squid, chicken, rice, veggies, shrimp, fruit, eggs, pancakes, and too many other things to list.
